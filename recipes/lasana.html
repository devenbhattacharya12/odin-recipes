Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • Italian Seasoning

For the cheese mixture:

  • egg
  • cheese
  • parsley

For Assembling:

  • Noodles
  • Extra cheese

Instructions

  1. Make the Sauce
  2. Assemble the Lasagna Layers
  3. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes
  4. Remove when bubbling, let cool, serve with salad
