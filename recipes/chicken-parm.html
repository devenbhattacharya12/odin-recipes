Chicken Parm Recipe

Ingredients

For the saurce sauce:

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • Italian Seasoning

For the chicken:

  • thinly sliced chicken breast

For Assembling:

  • parmesan cheese
  • mozzerella cheese

Instructions

  1. Make the Sauce
  2. Bread the chicken
  3. Fry the chicken
  4. Sauce the chicken
  5. Cheese the chicken
  6. Bake at 420 for 20 minutes
  7. Remove when bubbling, let cool, serve with salad
